Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Liam Delap Fan Club (“we”, “our”) runs this website and a community Discord server. This page explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

This website is mostly static. We do not run accounts or checkout on this site. If you join our Discord server, Discord’s own privacy policy applies to your account, messages, and activity there.

Cookies & analytics

We do not set marketing cookies on this site. GitHub Pages may log basic technical data (such as IP address and browser type) as part of hosting the site.

Third-party links

Links to Discord or other sites leave this website. Read their policies before sharing personal information there.

Children

Our community is not directed at children under 13. Discord’s minimum age rules still apply when you use their platform.

Changes

We may update this page from time to time. The date at the top will change when we do.

Questions

For privacy questions about the fan club, reach out to server staff on Discord after you join via our invite link.
